About Us

Vabrise Technology is a software development company in Nandi-Kenya. We provide ICT consultancy services and Software Development in the fields of corporate and financial management solutions, management, and energy.

Established in 2020, Vabrise Technologies has provided a wide range of ICT consultancy services and software development for the property management, human resource management, project management, energy monitoring, financial and accounting, mobile and value added services and supply chain management industries.
